What is pawnshop?

A jewelry pawnshop is an organization that issues short-term loans secured by precious metal products. First of all, jewelry is brought here, both new and worn. Also in some companies you can donate dishes, household items, souvenirs, coins. You can be sure that here you will not be denied the issuance of money if it is confirmed that the product is really valuable. The pawnshop does not check credit history and does not require proof of income. You receive money on the same day.

But not all pawnshops will be able to offer you favorable loan terms. Most of these firms evaluate precious metals by weight and fineness, without taking into account other equally important criteria - brand, historical significance, condition of the item, year of manufacture, precious stones. You receive the minimum amount. Therefore, it is better to choose companies that evaluate the product comprehensively. This method is used by the "Uvelirka jewelry pawnshop.
